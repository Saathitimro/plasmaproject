Requirements to become a Plasma Donor

  1. You must be tested COVID-19 positive in past
  2. You must be fully recovered from the COVID-19 disease and not taking any antibiotics
  3. You mustn't show any covid symptoms from past 14 days
  4. Your age must be between 18 to 60
  5. Your weight must be more than 50 kgs
  6. You mustn't have donated blood or blood components including plasma in past 28 days
  7. You must be in good health and feeling good at the time of donation
  8. You must not have have any Sexually transmitted disease , Cancer , Hepatitis B or Hepatitis C
  9. You will consult with doctor if you have diabeties or hyper tension
